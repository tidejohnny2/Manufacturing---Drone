Purchasing Policy

EOQ = √(2DS/H): D = annual demand (planned builds × BOM usage), S = ordering cost per PO, H = carrying rate × unit price. Reorder point = daily demand × (lead time + safety stock days). A part suggests an order when available stock falls to its min level or reorder point, whichever is higher.
