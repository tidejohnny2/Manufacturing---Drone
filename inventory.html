Inventory Areas

Parts Receiving + Kitting

Raw components are received, inspected, stored, and pulled into complete kits before work starts.

Production Station WIP

Partially built units move through Airframe, Electronics, Firmware, Motor Test, QA, and Packaged.

Sub-assembly Case Inventory

Finished transport cases from the case line are stocked here and pulled at packaging.

Finished goods FG Inventory

Accepted packaged units are stored as available stock after final scan and location assignment.

Rework Hold inventory

Failed or blocked units are held outside normal flow until disposition, repair, and release are complete.
